ABOUT TRAINCRAFT

Traincraft has been around since 2011 providing trains and rollingstock for minecraft.
During 1.6.4 the original developers Spitfire4466 and Mr. Brutal no longer had time to maintain the mod, and eventually opened it to the community.
Since then a new team has taken over, not only maintaining it and adding additional content, but rewriting the entire mod from scratch for future game versions.
It's been a long and bumpy road, but things have come a long way, Spitfire even came back as an advisor, and things will only get better from here.

COMMUNITY


We have a very active community, one of the best in minecraft. And we do a lot to keep them included in what's going on.
Feel free to check out our public discord and come say "hi" to the team, feel free to ask any questions. If there's any news on the mod, it is usually posted here first.
It's also the only place to easily find EternalBlueFlame, aside from the git.

If you're looking for a server to join, we try and keep an official list of all the known servers, feel free to check them out, leave comments, reviews, or even request your own to be added.
